Frequently Asked Question's on Labor Probability

1. What are the odds of going into labor at 37 weeks?

The odds of going into labor between 37 to 38 weeks is 26%.

2. What are 3 signs that labor is approaching?

The different signs of labor is coming soon are bloody show, water breaks, regular contractions, baby drops, lower back pain and cramping.

3. How likely is it to go into labor at 39 weeks?

In general, 25% gave birth by 39 weeks and 5 days after the LMP.

4. Can you predict when you will go into labor?

The truth is there is no way to predict exactly when one will go into labor. No one knows for sure what triggers the big event, although hormones play a role. There are at least 6 clues that your baby is preparing to make entry into the world.
