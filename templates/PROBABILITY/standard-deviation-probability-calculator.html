Standard Deviation Probability Formula

Standard deviation in probability is nothing but the average distance of a random variable which is taken from the mean. Standard deviation is denoted by 'σ'. To find the probability of standard deviation we use a certain formula, let us look into it:

σ = √Σ(xi-μ)2 * P(xi)

Where,

μ is the mean.

P is the pobability.

xi is the ith value.

P(xi) is the probability of ith value.

Standard Deviation Probability Solved Example

Example: Find the probability of standard deviation for the following data.

Goals(x) Probability P(x)
1 0.56
2 0.16

Solution:

Given values are x =1, 2 and P(x) = 0.56, 0.16

First, we need to calculate the mean for the above table values.

Mean (μ ) = 1 * (0.56) + 2 * (0.16) = 0.88.

Now, calculating (xi-μ)2 * P(xi)

P(0.56) = (1-0.88)2 * 0.56 = 0.008064.

P(0.16) = (1-0.88)2 * 0.16 = 0.002304

Formula, σ = √Σ(xi-μ)2 * P(xi)

Standard deviation probability = √(0.008064 + 0.002304) = 0.101

Therefore, the standard deviation of probability is 0.101
