How to Calculate the Probability of Cards?

Usually, when you are drawing cards, you want to know your odds you need to use something called hypergeometric distribution, i.e. finding probability of cards. Let us use the formula to find the probability of cards.

H (n) = C (X, n) * C (Y – X, Z – n) / C (Y, Z)

Where,

X is the no. of a certain card in the deck.

Y is the total no. of cards in the deck.

Z is the no. of cards drawn

N is the number that you are checking for
