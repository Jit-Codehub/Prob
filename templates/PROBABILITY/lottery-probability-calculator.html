Lottery Formula

Here we are giving the lottery formula that helps to find the winning chances of the lottery.

Lottery = C(n,r) / [C(r,m) * C(n-r, r-m)]

Where,

m is the number of balls to be drawn

n is the number of matches

r is the number of balls in pool

FAQ's on Lottery Odds Calculator

1. Is it possible to calculate lottery numbers?

The only assumption to calculate lottery probability is each number in the lottery has an equal probability of being chosen as the winning number. Actually, there is no tool that predicts the exact winning lottery number.

2. How to calculate the probability of winning the lottery?

To find the odds of winning any lottery, divide the number of winning lottery numbers by the total number of possible lottery numbers. You can also use the lottery formula to calculate the probability of winning a lottery.

3. How to find probability?

To find the probability of an event, you have to know the total number of possible outcomes that can occur. Divide the number of events by the total number of possible outcomes to get the probability value.

4. What is the lottery formula?

The formula to calculate lottery odds is x = C(n,r) / [C(r,m) * C(n-r, r-m)]. Where n is the number of balls in the pool, r is the number of balls to be drawn and m is the expected number of matches.
