Coin Flip Probability Example

Example

Question: Find the probability of obtaining 4 heads when you flip a coin 10 times?

Solution

Number of times you need to flip a coin (n) = 10

Total Possible outcomes = 2n= 210=2x2x2x2x2x2x2x2x2x2 = 1024.

Total no.of favourable outcomes = 10C4 = 201

No.of heads = 4.

Let us use the probability formula,

Probability = no of favorable outcomes/ total number of possible outcomes

probability = 201/1024.
